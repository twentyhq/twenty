## Pricing Plans

Twenty offers flexible pricing to fit teams of all sizes.

### Free (Self-Hosted)
Host Twenty on your own infrastructure at no cost:
- All Pro features included
- Unlimited users
- Community support via Discord
- Full control over your data

### Pro
For teams ready to scale:
- All core CRM features
- Email and calendar sync
- Workflows and automations
- 10 million workflow credits/month
- Standard support

### Organization
For larger teams with advanced needs:
- Everything in Pro
- 20 million workflow credits/month
- SSO integration
- Advanced permissions
- Priority support

## Workflow Credits

Credits power your workflow automations:

| Plan | Monthly Credits |
|------|-----------------|
| Trial | 5 million |
| Pro | 10 million |
| Organization | 20 million |

### Credit Consumption
Different actions consume different amounts of credits:
- **Basic operations** (search, update, create): Minimal credits
- **Complex operations** (code nodes, external API calls): More credits
- **AI prompts** (coming soon): Variable based on usage

### Purchasing Additional Credits
Need more credits? Purchase additional packs under **Settings → Billing**.

## Switching Plans

### Upgrade to Organization
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Switch to Organization**
3. Confirm your upgrade

### Downgrade to Pro
Contact support to downgrade your plan.

### Switch to Yearly Billing
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Switch to Yearly**
3. Save with annual billing
