## Accessing Billing Settings

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. View your current plan and usage
3. Access billing management options

## Changing Your Plan

### Upgrade to Organization
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Switch to Organization**
3. Review the features and pricing
4. Confirm your upgrade
5. New plan activates immediately

### Downgrade to Pro
Contact support to downgrade your plan. This cannot be done through the UI currently.

## Billing Cycle

### Switch to Yearly Billing
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **Switch to Yearly**
3. Review the annual pricing (with discount)
4. Confirm the change
5. Billing cycle updates

### Switch to Monthly Billing
Contact support to switch from yearly to monthly billing.

## Managing Payment

### Update Payment Method
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **View billing details**
3. Navigate to payment methods
4. Click **Add payment method**
5. Enter new card details
6. Save changes

### Update Billing Information
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **View billing details**
3. Edit billing information:
   - Company name
   - Address
   - VAT number
4. Save changes

## Workflow Credits

### View Credit Usage
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. See current credit balance
3. View usage history

### Purchase Additional Credits
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Find the credits section
3. Select a credit package
4. Complete purchase
5. Credits added immediately

## Invoices

### Access Invoices
1. Go to **Settings → Billing**
2. Click **View billing details**
3. Scroll to invoices section
4. Click to download any invoice

### Invoice Information
Invoices include:
- Subscription charges
- Credit purchases
- Usage details
- Payment information

## Managing Seats

### Add Users
Adding users may affect your billing:
- Pro and Organization plans are per-user
- New users are added to your next invoice
- Prorated charges may apply

### Remove Users
1. Go to **Settings → Members**
2. Remove the user
3. Billing adjusts on next cycle

## Cancellation

### Cancel Subscription
To cancel your subscription:
1. Contact support
2. Specify your cancellation date
3. Data is retained for a grace period
4. Export any needed data before expiration

<Warning>
Cancellation will result in loss of access to all workspace data. Export your data before canceling.
</Warning>
