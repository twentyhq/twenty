## Joining the Discord Community

### Getting Started

**Join the Server**:
- Visit [discord.gg/cx5n4Jzs57](https://discord.gg/cx5n4Jzs57) to join
- Create a Discord account if you don't have one
- Read and accept the community guidelines
- Introduce yourself in the welcome channel

**Setting Up Your Profile**:
- Add a profile picture and display name
- Set your status to let others know your role (user, developer, etc.)
- Enable notifications for channels you want to follow closely
- Customize your notification settings to avoid spam

## Getting Help on Discord

### Best Practices for Asking Questions

**Be Specific and Clear**:
- Describe your problem in detail
- Include relevant error messages or screenshots
- Mention your Twenty version and environment
- Explain what you've already tried

**Use the Right Channel**:
- #help-general for basic usage questions
- #help-technical for complex technical issues
- #help-self-hosted for deployment questions
- #api-webhooks for integration problems

**Search Before Asking**:
- Use Discord's search function to look for similar questions
- Check pinned messages in channels for common solutions
- Review recent conversations for related discussions
- Look at the documentation before asking

### Example of a Good Help Request

```
**Problem**: Email sync not working with Gmail
**Environment**: Twenty Cloud, Chrome browser
**Error**: "Authentication failed" when trying to connect Gmail
**Steps tried**:
1. Cleared browser cache
2. Tried incognito mode
3. Checked Gmail settings for IMAP access
**Screenshots**: [attached error message screenshot]
```

### Getting Faster Responses

**Timing Matters**:
- The community is global, so responses come at different times
- Peak activity is typically during US and European business hours
- Be patient - community members volunteer their time to help
- Consider time zones when expecting responses

**Engage with the Community**:
- Help others when you can - it builds goodwill
- Share your solutions when you solve problems
- Participate in discussions beyond just asking for help
- Thank people who help you

## Community Guidelines and Etiquette

### Code of Conduct

**Be Respectful**:
- Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination
- Keep discussions constructive and professional
- Respect different opinions and approaches

**Stay On Topic**:
- Keep discussions relevant to the channel purpose
- Use appropriate channels for different types of content
- Avoid excessive off-topic conversations in help channels
- Move lengthy discussions to appropriate channels

**No Spam or Self-Promotion**:
- Avoid excessive self-promotion or advertising
- Don't spam channels with repeated messages
- Share relevant content that benefits the community
- Get permission before sharing external links or resources

### Communication Best Practices

**Clear Communication**:
- Use clear, concise language
- Format code and error messages properly using Discord's formatting
- Use threads for extended discussions to keep channels organized
- Tag people appropriately when seeking specific help

**Helpful Behavior**:
- Search before asking duplicate questions
- Thank people who help you
- Share solutions when you find them
- Be patient with response times
