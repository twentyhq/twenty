## Key features

- **Table views**: Spreadsheet-like views with customizable columns
- **Kanban boards**: Visual boards for tracking deals and projects through stages
- **Filters & sorting**: Save views with specific filters and sort orders
- **Pipeline management**: Track opportunities through customizable sales stages
- **View sharing**: Share views with your team or keep them private
