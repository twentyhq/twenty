## What is Twenty's AI Assistant?

Twenty's AI assistant is an intelligent chatbot built directly into your CRM that can help you with various tasks, answer questions about your data, and provide insights about your business processes.

## Core Capabilities

### Data Analysis and Insights
The AI assistant can analyze your CRM data to provide valuable insights:

**Customer Analysis**: Get summaries of customer interactions, identify patterns in communication, and understand relationship health.

**Sales Insights**: Analyze opportunity pipelines, identify bottlenecks, and get recommendations for improving conversion rates.

**Activity Summaries**: Review recent activities, upcoming tasks, and important deadlines across your workspace.

**Performance Metrics**: Get quick answers about team performance, deal values, and activity levels.

### Question Answering
Ask the AI assistant questions about your CRM data and processes:

**Record Information**: "What's the status of the Acme Corp deal?" or "When did I last contact John Smith?"

**Process Guidance**: "How do I create a custom field?" or "What's the best way to set up email sync?"

**Data Queries**: "Show me all opportunities over $50,000" or "Which companies haven't been contacted in 30 days?"

**System Help**: Get explanations of features, settings, and best practices.

### Task Assistance
The AI can help you accomplish common CRM tasks:

**Record Creation**: Guide you through creating contacts, companies, or opportunities with the right information.

**Workflow Suggestions**: Recommend automation workflows based on your business processes.

**Data Cleanup**: Identify duplicate records, incomplete information, or data quality issues.

**Integration Help**: Assist with connecting external tools and configuring integrations.

## How It Works

### Contextual Understanding
The AI assistant understands the context of your current workspace:
- Knows about your custom objects and fields
- Understands your team structure and permissions
- Recognizes your business processes and workflows
- Adapts responses to your industry and use case

### Data Privacy and Security
Your data privacy is protected:
- AI only accesses data within your workspace permissions
- Conversations are not stored permanently
- No data is shared with external AI training
- All interactions follow your workspace security settings

### Learning and Adaptation
The AI assistant becomes more helpful over time:
- Learns from your questions and preferences
- Adapts to your team's terminology and processes
- Improves suggestions based on usage patterns
- Updates knowledge with new Twenty features

## Accessing the AI Assistant

### Chat Interface
Open the AI chat through multiple methods:
- Click the AI icon in the bottom-right corner
- Use the command palette (Cmd+K) and select "Ask AI"
- Navigate through the main menu to AI features
- Use keyboard shortcuts for quick access

### Integration Points
The AI assistant appears in various contexts:
- Main dashboard for general questions
- Record pages for specific data queries
- Workflow builder for automation suggestions
- Settings pages for configuration help

## AI Model Options

### Model Selection
Twenty offers different AI models for various needs:

**Fast Model**: Quick responses for simple questions and basic tasks
- Optimized for speed and efficiency
- Best for straightforward queries
- Lower resource usage

**Smart Model**: Advanced reasoning for complex analysis and insights
- Better understanding of context and nuance
- Superior for data analysis and recommendations
- More sophisticated problem-solving capabilities

### Usage and Credits
AI usage may consume workflow credits depending on your plan:
- Different models have different credit costs
- Monitor usage through your billing dashboard
- Optimize model selection based on query complexity
- Consider upgrading plans for heavy AI usage

## Best Practices

### Effective Communication
**Be Specific**: Ask clear, specific questions for better responses
- Instead of "How are sales?" ask "What's our conversion rate this month?"
- Provide context when asking about specific records or processes

**Use Natural Language**: Communicate as you would with a colleague
- No need for special syntax or commands
- Ask follow-up questions to clarify or expand on responses

**Provide Context**: Help the AI understand your specific situation
- Mention your role, team, or department when relevant
- Reference specific time periods, goals, or challenges

### Data Quality
**Keep Data Updated**: The AI's insights are only as good as your data
- Regularly update contact information and deal statuses
- Maintain accurate activity logs and communication records
- Clean up duplicate or outdated records

**Use Consistent Terminology**: Help the AI understand your business language
- Standardize field names and values across your team
- Document custom processes and terminology
- Train team members on consistent data entry

## Limitations and Considerations

### Current Limitations
**Data Scope**: AI can only access data within your workspace permissions

**Real-Time Updates**: Responses may not reflect the very latest data changes

**Complex Calculations**: Some advanced analytics may require manual analysis

**External Data**: Cannot access information outside your Twenty workspace

### Privacy Considerations
**Sensitive Information**: Be mindful of confidential data in conversations

**Team Access**: Consider who can access AI chat logs and insights

**Compliance**: Ensure AI usage aligns with your data governance policies

**Audit Trails**: Monitor AI interactions for compliance and security
