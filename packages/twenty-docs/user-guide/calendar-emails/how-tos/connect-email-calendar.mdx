## Connect Google Account

### Steps
1. Go to **Settings → Accounts**
2. Click **Add account**
3. Select **Continue with Google**
4. Sign in to your Google account
5. Review and accept the permissions
6. Your Gmail and Google Calendar will start syncing

### What Gets Synced
- **Emails**: All external emails (internal emails excluded)
- **Calendar**: Meetings with external participants
- **Contacts**: Optionally auto-create contacts from emails

## Connect Microsoft Account

### Steps
1. Go to **Settings → Accounts**
2. Click **Add account**
3. Select **Continue with Microsoft**
4. Sign in to your Microsoft account
5. Review and accept the permissions
6. Your Outlook and Calendar will start syncing

### What Gets Synced
- **Emails**: All external emails (internal emails excluded)
- **Calendar**: Meetings with external participants
- **Contacts**: Optionally auto-create contacts from emails

## Configure Sync Settings

### Email Visibility
Choose what's visible to your team:
1. Go to the connected account settings
2. Select visibility level:
   - **Metadata Only**: Date, time, participants
   - **Subject + Metadata**: Add subject line
   - **Everything**: Full email content

### Contact Auto-Creation
Control whether contacts are created from emails:
1. Go to the connected account settings
2. Choose auto-creation option:
   - **Deactivated**: No auto-creation
   - **Sent & Received**: Create from all emails
   - **Sent Only**: Create only from emails you send

### Folder Selection (Lab Feature)
Control which folders sync:
1. Enable **Message Folder** in Settings → Releases → Lab
2. Return to account settings
3. Select folders to include/exclude

## Troubleshooting

### Emails Not Appearing
- Wait 5 minutes for initial sync
- Check if the contact exists in Twenty
- Verify folder sync settings
- Ensure it's not an internal email (same domain)

### Calendar Not Syncing
- Verify calendar permissions were granted
- Check that meetings have external participants
- Wait for initial sync to complete

### Authorization Issues
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting
- Clear browser cache
- Check if your admin allows third-party apps
