## Key features

- **Automatic email linking**: Emails are automatically linked to People, Companies, and Opportunities
- **Calendar sync**: View all meetings related to a contact or company
- **Multiple accounts**: Connect multiple email accounts per user
- **Privacy controls**: Choose what's visible to your team (metadata only, subject, or full content)
- **Contact auto-creation**: Optionally create contacts from email interactions
