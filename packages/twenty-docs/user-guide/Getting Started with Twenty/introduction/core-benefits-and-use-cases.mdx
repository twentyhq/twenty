## Why Choose Twenty CRM?

Twenty CRM delivers powerful benefits that help businesses of all sizes improve their customer relationships, streamline operations, and drive growth.

## Core Benefits

### Complete Data Control
Your customer data stays under your control. With Twenty's open-source architecture, you can:
- Host your data wherever you want
- Export everything at any time
- Customize without vendor restrictions
- Integrate with any system you need

### Unlimited Customization
Build the exact CRM your business needs:
- Create custom objects for any business entity
- Add fields that match your data requirements
- Design workflows that automate your processes
- Configure views that show information your way

### Powerful Automation
Reduce manual work and eliminate errors:
- Automate data entry and updates
- Trigger actions based on business rules
- Sync information between systems
- Send notifications and reminders automatically

### Team Collaboration
Keep everyone aligned and productive:
- Share views and insights across teams
- Track all customer interactions in one place
- Assign tasks and monitor progress
- Maintain a complete activity history

### Scalable Architecture
Grow without limitations:
- Handle increasing data volumes
- Add users without per-seat restrictions
- Expand functionality through APIs
- Integrate with enterprise systems

## Common Use Cases

### Sales Teams

**Lead Management**: Capture leads from multiple sources, qualify prospects, and track them through your sales process.

**Opportunity Tracking**: Monitor deals from initial contact to close, with clear visibility into pipeline health and forecasting.

**Account Management**: Maintain detailed customer profiles, track interaction history, and identify expansion opportunities.

**Sales Automation**: Automate follow-up sequences, task creation, and data updates to keep deals moving forward.

### Customer Success Teams

**Onboarding Tracking**: Guide new customers through implementation with structured processes and milestone tracking.

**Health Monitoring**: Track customer engagement, usage patterns, and satisfaction to identify at-risk accounts.

**Renewal Management**: Monitor contract dates, track renewal conversations, and automate renewal workflows.

**Support Integration**: Connect support tickets to customer records for complete service history visibility.

### Marketing Teams

**Campaign Management**: Track campaign performance, lead attribution, and ROI across different marketing channels.

**Lead Nurturing**: Segment prospects based on behavior and demographics, then deliver targeted content sequences.

**Event Management**: Manage event attendees, track follow-up activities, and measure event impact on pipeline.

**Content Tracking**: Monitor which content resonates with different audience segments and optimize accordingly.

### Service Organizations

**Project Management**: Track projects from proposal to completion, managing resources, timelines, and deliverables.

**Client Communication**: Maintain detailed communication logs, meeting notes, and project updates in one place.

**Resource Planning**: Allocate team members to projects based on skills, availability, and workload.

**Invoice Tracking**: Connect project milestones to billing cycles and track payment status.

### Real Estate Teams

**Property Management**: Track properties, owners, tenants, and maintenance requests in custom objects.

**Lead Qualification**: Score and prioritize leads based on budget, timeline, and property preferences.

**Transaction Tracking**: Monitor deals through inspection, financing, and closing stages.

**Referral Management**: Track referral sources and maintain relationships with past clients for future business.

### Consulting Firms

**Opportunity Pipeline**: Track potential engagements from initial inquiry through contract signing.

**Client Relationship Management**: Maintain detailed profiles of decision makers, project history, and preferences.

**Proposal Tracking**: Monitor proposal status, follow-up activities, and win/loss analysis.

**Knowledge Management**: Store project insights, methodologies, and best practices for team access.

## Getting the Most Value

To maximize your success with Twenty:

1. **Start Simple**: Begin with basic objects and fields, then expand as you learn
2. **Involve Your Team**: Get input from users who will work with the system daily
3. **Plan Your Data Model**: Think through your processes before building complex structures
4. **Automate Gradually**: Start with simple workflows and add complexity over time
5. **Measure Results**: Track how Twenty improves your key business metrics
