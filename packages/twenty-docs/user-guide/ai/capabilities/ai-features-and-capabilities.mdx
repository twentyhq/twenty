## AI in Twenty

Twenty integrates AI capabilities to help you work smarter and more efficiently with your CRM data.

## AI Chat

### What It Does
AI Chat allows you to interact with your CRM data using natural language. Ask questions, get insights, and find information without navigating through menus.

### How to Use It
1. Click the AI icon in the navigation
2. Type your question in natural language
3. The AI analyzes your data and responds

### Example Queries
- "Show me all opportunities closing this month"
- "Find contacts at healthcare companies"
- "What's the total pipeline value?"
- "List companies I haven't contacted in 30 days"

## AI Agents

### What They Are
AI agents are configurable assistants that can automate tasks and provide intelligent insights based on your CRM data.

### Capabilities
- **Data Analysis**: Identify patterns and trends in your data
- **Task Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks
- **Smart Recommendations**: Get suggestions based on your workflow

### AI-Powered Actions
Integrate AI into your workflows for:
- **Data Enrichment**: Automatically enhance records
- **Classification**: Categorize records based on content
- **Summarization**: Generate summaries from text fields
- **Smart Routing**: Route records based on AI analysis

## Best Practices

### Getting Good Results
- **Be specific**: Clear questions get better answers
- **Provide context**: Include relevant details in your queries
- **Iterate**: Refine your questions based on results

### Data Quality
- **Clean data**: AI works best with well-organized data
- **Complete records**: Fill in key fields for better analysis
- **Consistent naming**: Use consistent naming conventions

### Security
- AI respects your permission settings
- Data is processed securely
- Configure access controls appropriately

## Current Limitations

- AI features are being actively developed
- Some capabilities require specific plan levels
- Complex queries may need refinement
