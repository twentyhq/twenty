## Enabling AI Features

AI features in Twenty help you work more efficiently by providing intelligent assistance throughout your CRM workflows.

### Prerequisites
- An active Twenty workspace
- Appropriate permissions to access AI features
- AI features enabled in your workspace settings

### Enable AI
1. Go to **Settings → Releases → Lab**
2. Find the AI features section
3. Toggle on the AI features you want to use
4. Return to your workspace to start using AI

## AI Chat

The AI Chat allows you to interact with your CRM data using natural language.

### How to Use AI Chat
1. Click on the AI icon in the navigation
2. Type your question or command
3. The AI will analyze your data and provide insights

### Example Queries
- "Show me all opportunities closing this month"
- "Find contacts at companies in the healthcare industry"
- "Summarize my recent activity with Acme Corp"

## AI in Workflows

AI can be integrated into your workflows to automate intelligent decision-making:

- **Data enrichment**: Automatically enhance records with AI-generated insights
- **Classification**: Categorize records based on content
- **Summarization**: Generate summaries from lengthy content

## Best Practices

- **Be specific**: Clear, specific questions get better results
- **Iterate**: Refine your queries based on the responses you get
- **Verify**: Always verify AI-generated information before acting on it
