## Advanced Sales Capabilities

GTM teams often need advanced sales capabilities like lead scoring, data enrichment, round robin, territory assignment, automated reminders, and email sequences. Selv om dette ikke er innebygde funksjoner i Twenty, kan de alle konfigureres og tilpasses dine spesifikke behov ved å bruke vårt fleksible arbeidsflytsystem.

Visit our [Workflows section](/l/no/user-guide/workflows/getting-started-workflows) to learn how to build these automations step by step. For detailed examples, see our [Internal Automations](/l/no/user-guide/workflows/internal-automations) and [External Tool Integration](/l/no/user-guide/workflows/external-tool-integration) guides.
