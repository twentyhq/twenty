## What's Coming

### Brugerdefinerede rapporter

Opret skræddersyede rapporter for at spore de målinger, der betyder mest for din virksomhed.

### Salgsdashboards

Visualize your sales pipeline performance with interactive dashboards.

### Præstationsanalyse

Overvåg holdpræstationer og identificér tendenser i dine kundedata.

## Hold dig opdateret

Follow our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/twentyhq/twenty) or check the **Settings → Releases** section in your workspace to get notified when reporting features become available.
