## Snapshot

- **Product:** Open-source CRM — "the open alternative to Salesforce, designed for AI."
- **Founded:** 2023 · **YC batch:** Summer 2023 (S23) · YC partner: Pete Koomen.
- **HQ:** San Francisco (official, YC) — but French founders, strong Paris presence; press calls them "Paris-based."
- **License:** AGPLv3 (permissive enough to fork / white-label — basis of our `tofu/` setup).
- **Repo:** https://github.com/twentyhq/twenty (created Dec 2022).

**Origin story (content gold):** The three previously built **Luckey**, a property-management app /
mini-CRM for vacation rentals, **acquired by Airbnb**. The CRM idea traces to ~2015 when Félix ran a
property-management business, evaluated Salesforce, and walked away — he didn't want to be "hostage to a
closed-source project," so he built his own. After selling Luckey to Airbnb he kept returning to the
problem. His framing: *"CRM is the biggest software market overall, because it covers marketing, customer
support, operations — a CRM does everything."* The "don't be a hostage → own your stack" thread runs
through their brand today.

## Brand & positioning (what we'd align with)

- **Taglines:** "The open alternative to Salesforce, designed for AI" · "#1 Open Source CRM."
- **Brand thesis ([twenty.com/story](https://twenty.com/story)):** "The future of CRM is built, not bought."
  / "Differentiation now lives in the code you own."
- **AI / dev angle (directly relevant to our flywheel):** their story page states *"A developer can describe
  what they want to Claude Code and have a working app in an afternoon."* They actively court builders who
  customize and ship on top of Twenty — the wedge for both our app-dev and content pitches.

## Partnership read & next actions

- **No public formal partner/reseller program found** — good news. A community-driven, Series-A-stage,
  builder-obsessed company is more likely to say yes to a hands-on agency partner than a mature vendor with
  a rigid channel program.
- **Best path:** direct founder contact (Charles most active on LinkedIn; Félix on X) + show up in Discord
  with real contributions/content.
- **Pitch in their language:** TOFU service that gets clients onto an owned, open CRM → custom apps on top →
  ecosystem content. Frame around "built, not bought."
