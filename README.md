# Why Twenty

AI turned CRM into the system that runs your go-to-market, and every team on the same SaaS gets the same agent decisions. Twenty is the CRM you build, ship, and version like the rest of your stack.

<br />

# What You Can Do With Twenty

Twenty v2 is a code-first, AI-native CRM platform. Build, ship, and version your own CRM apps — with the full CRM toolkit out of the box and AI agents on top.

# Stack
- [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
- [Nx](https://nx.dev/)
- [NestJS](https://nestjs.com/), with [BullMQ](https://bullmq.io/), [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org/), [Redis](https://redis.io/)
- [React](https://reactjs.org/), with [Jotai](https://jotai.org/), [Linaria](https://linaria.dev/) and [Lingui](https://lingui.dev/)
